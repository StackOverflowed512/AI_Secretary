Continuous Learning Pipeline

1

Capture: You feed data through emails, files, or direct notes. Seva-Sakha automatically extracts the core concepts.

2

Vectorize: Data is transformed into mathematical vectors using Mistral Embeddings and stored in your local persistent memory.

3

Recall: During chat, the assistant queries this memory in real-time, providing answers based on *your* specific data.
